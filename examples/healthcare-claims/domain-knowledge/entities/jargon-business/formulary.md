# Formulary

## Definition

Structured list of covered prescription medications organized into tiers (Tier 1 generics, Tier 2 preferred brand, Tier 3 non-preferred brand, Tier 4 specialty) with different cost-sharing per tier.

## Context

During pharmacy adjudication, the system checks the formulary tier for the prescribed drug. Non-formulary drugs may be denied or require prior authorization. Specialty drugs often need both pre-authorization and utilization review.

The formulary is managed by the PBM (Pharmacy Benefit Manager) team and updated quarterly.

## Example

Physician prescribes brand-name cholesterol medication (Tier 2, $40 copay). Generic equivalent is Tier 1 ($10). Member portal shows both options.
