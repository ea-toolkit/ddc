# Copay

## Definition

Predetermined flat fee member pays for a covered service, typically collected at point of service. Varies by service type (e.g., $30 PCP, $50 specialist, $15 generic drugs).

## Context

Unlike deductibles, copays are per-visit fixed amounts, not cumulative thresholds. During adjudication, the rules engine applies the copay from the member's benefit plan for the service category. Copays generally count toward the out-of-pocket maximum but not toward the deductible.

## Example

Plan has $30 PCP copay. Member visits PCP, provider collects $30. Claim submitted for $150. Plan pays allowed amount minus $30.
