# Capitation

## Definition

Provider reimbursement model with fixed per-member-per-month (PMPM) payment for all contracted services regardless of utilization. Shifts financial risk to the provider.

## Context

Under capitation, providers still submit encounter claims for tracking purposes, but these claims are not paid on a per-claim basis. The claims system must distinguish fee-for-service claims from capitated encounters.

Some services may be carved out of capitation and paid on a fee-for-service basis. Monthly reconciliation handles membership changes (new enrollees, disenrollments).

## Example

Medical group capitated at $50 PMPM for 10,000 members ($500K/month). Member visits PCP — encounter claim processed for tracking, no additional payment generated.
