# Allowed Amount

## Definition

Maximum amount the plan considers payable for a service. In-network: negotiated contract rate. Out-of-network: fee schedule benchmark (e.g., percentage of Medicare). Member cost-sharing is calculated against the allowed amount, not the billed amount.

## Context

The allowed amount is determined during adjudication. In-network providers accept the allowed amount as payment in full, meaning they cannot balance-bill the member. Out-of-network providers may balance-bill the member for amounts exceeding the allowed amount.

## Example

Provider bills $500. In-network allowed amount is $350. Provider writes off $150. Plan pays 80% of $350 ($280), member pays 20% ($70).
