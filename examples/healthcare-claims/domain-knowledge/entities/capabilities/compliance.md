# Compliance

## Overview

Regulatory compliance, fraud prevention, audit management, and HIPAA data privacy. Ensures the plan operates within regulatory requirements and protects against fraud, waste, and abuse.

## Sub-Capabilities

- **Regulatory Compliance** — Adherence to CMS, state DOI, and ACA requirements
- **Fraud Detection** — Pre-payment and post-payment fraud, waste, and abuse detection
- **Audit Management** — Internal and external audit support, documentation, and remediation
- **Data Privacy** — HIPAA compliance, PHI protection, and access controls

## Key Systems

- Fraud Detection — ML-based FWA detection and SIU case management
