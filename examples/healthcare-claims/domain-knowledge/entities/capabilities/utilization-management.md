# Utilization Management

## Overview

Medical necessity review, prior authorization, concurrent review, and care coordination. Ensures healthcare services are medically necessary and appropriate while managing costs.

## Sub-Capabilities

- **Prior Authorization Management** — Processing and managing pre-authorization requests
- **Concurrent Review** — Evaluating ongoing inpatient stays against clinical criteria
- **Retrospective Review** — Post-service review of claims for medical necessity
- **Care Coordination** — Coordinating care transitions and managing complex cases

## Key Systems

- Pre-Auth Service — authorization request processing and determination
