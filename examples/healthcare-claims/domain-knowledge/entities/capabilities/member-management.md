# Member Management

## Overview

Member enrollment, eligibility verification, benefits administration, and communications. Ensures members are properly enrolled, their benefits are correctly configured, and they have access to self-service tools.

## Sub-Capabilities

- **Member Enrollment** — Processing new enrollments, changes, and terminations
- **Eligibility Verification** — Real-time verification of member coverage and benefits
- **Benefits Administration** — Managing benefit plan assignments and accumulator tracking
- **Member Communications** — EOBs, ID cards, benefit summaries, and portal notifications

## Key Systems

- Eligibility Service — enrollment, eligibility verification, and accumulator tracking
- Member Portal — self-service web and mobile application
