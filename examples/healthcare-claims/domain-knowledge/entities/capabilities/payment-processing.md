# Payment Processing

## Overview

Provider payments, member reimbursements, remittance advice generation, and reconciliation. Translates adjudicated claims into financial transactions and ensures accurate, timely payment.

## Sub-Capabilities

- **Provider Payments** — Batching and disbursing payments to providers via EFT, check, or virtual card
- **Member Reimbursements** — Processing reimbursements for out-of-network claims submitted by members
- **Remittance Generation** — Generating ERA 835 electronic remittance advice and EOB documents
- **Payment Reconciliation** — Reconciling payments, handling voids/reissues, and managing recoupments

## Key Systems

- Payment Engine — payment batching, disbursement, remittance, and reconciliation
