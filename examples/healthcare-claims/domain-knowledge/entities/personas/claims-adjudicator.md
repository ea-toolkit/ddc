# Claims Adjudicator

## Role Description

Reviews and processes claims, handles exceptions, and manages pended claims requiring manual review. Configures adjudication rules in the rules engine and monitors auto-adjudication rates to ensure processing accuracy.

## Key Activities

- Review pended claims that require manual adjudication
- Configure and maintain business rules in the rules engine
- Investigate claim edits and resolve processing exceptions
- Manage provider disputes related to claim adjudication
- Monitor auto-adjudication rates and identify rule gaps
- Handle coordination of benefits scenarios requiring manual intervention

## Pain Points

- Complex COB scenarios requiring manual research across multiple systems
- Inconsistent provider data causing incorrect network determinations
- Rules engine currently being replaced with new vendor, requiring dual-system knowledge
- High volume of manual review queue due to rule gaps and edge cases
