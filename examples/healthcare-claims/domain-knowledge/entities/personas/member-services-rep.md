# Member Services Representative

## Role Description

Handles member inquiries about claims status, benefit coverage, provider network, and EOB explanations. First point of contact for members needing assistance understanding their benefits and claim outcomes.

## Key Activities

- Explain EOBs to members, including allowed amounts, member responsibility, and reason codes
- Resolve claim status inquiries and provide processing updates
- Verify member eligibility and benefit coverage for specific services
- Help members with out-of-network claim submissions
- Escalate complaints and grievances to appropriate teams
- Assist members with provider search and network questions

## Pain Points

- Complex EOB explanations when multiple cost-sharing components apply
- Members confused by the difference between allowed amount and billed amount
- COB scenarios requiring coordination with multiple plans and lengthy research
- Long call handle times due to navigating multiple systems for a single inquiry
