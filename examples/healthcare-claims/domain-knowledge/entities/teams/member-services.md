# Member Services

## Overview

Owns member enrollment, eligibility verification, self-service portal, and benefit administration. Responsible for member experience, accurate enrollment data, and timely eligibility processing.

## Responsibilities

- Process member enrollment, changes, and terminations
- Maintain accurate eligibility and benefit plan data
- Operate and enhance the member self-service portal
- Handle member inquiries and complaints
- Generate and distribute EOBs and member communications
- Manage coordination of benefits data
- Support open enrollment periods

## Systems Owned

- **Member Portal** — self-service web and mobile application for members
- **Eligibility Service** — enrollment, eligibility verification, and accumulator tracking
