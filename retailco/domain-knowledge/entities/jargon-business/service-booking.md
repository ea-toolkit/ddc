# ServiceBooking

## Overview

ServiceBooking refers to the capability for customers to book, confirm, and pay for services alongside their product orders. These are value-added services like installation services, product installation, in-home delivery and placement, and removal of old items.

## Details

### Service Categories
- **Assembly**: Installation services, assembly services (variable cost depending on complexity)
- **Installation**: Product-specific installations
- **Delivery**: In-home placement of new products
- **Removal**: Taking away old products

### Business Impact
- Customers prepay for services and receive confirmed appointments
- When services fail (e.g., stuck in ReadyToAssign), customers expect a provider who never arrives
- This directly damages brand trust and leads to cancellations and rescheduling
- Estimated impact of a single major incident: significant financial impact per major incident
